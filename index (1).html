Why is Daniel Caesar called daniel caesar

About HIM

Daniel caesar knows his music makes you cry Daniel Caesar (Ashton Simmonds), born April 5, 1995, is a Canadian Grammy-winning R&B singer-songwriter known for his soulful, gospel-influenced music and hits like "Best Part" (feat. H.E.R.) and "Get You". Rising from Toronto's music scene after a period of homelessness, he achieved fame with his 2017 debut album Freudian.
